About Us

Change to create and preserve, sustainable technology!!

It helps you to keep a check on how many environmental friendly products you use in your daily routine and gives you points for the same.'GREEN-STOP' is a web-app can help you understand and protect the environment,and guide you towards the small steps you can take each day to take more eco-friendly action and become more aware of your impact on the earth and own health

Facilities

Plant A Tree

For every 250 green-points, 'Green-Stop' provides you the opportunity to use these points to plant trees in one of the areas of the country in desperate need of vegetation.To use your green-points to plant trees, simply add Plant A Tree to your shopping basket from the Products Section and begin your Planting Journey with us!.

Public Transport

Switch to public transport and use more environment friendly options for your daily commute. For 1000 green-points , get a free ride pass from us and travel around the city on the transport of your choice .

Organic Products

By using this facility, 'Do your bit' and be part of a community that engages in the use of green and eco-friendly products. Use your green-points to purchase organic products and get interesting discounts. At the time of redemption, 100 Green Points = 1 Re. For every purchase from your local shops, one can avail of a 10% discount on the total purchase value .
